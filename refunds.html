Refund & Dispute Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2024

Last Updated: June 21, 2024

Our Commitment

We stand behind our service. If we fail to deliver, you don't pay.

Refund Policy

Full Refund Guaranteed If:

  • Submission Failure: We fail to submit your request to the government agency within 24 hours of payment
  • System Error: Technical issues on our end prevent request processing
  • Duplicate Charge: You were charged multiple times for the same request
  • Service Unavailable: The requested jurisdiction is not actually supported by our service

Partial Refunds Available For:

  • Significant Delays: If processing takes longer than 7 business days due to our error (50% refund)
  • Incomplete Service: If we can only partially fulfill your request (prorated refund)

No Refunds For:

  • Government agency delays beyond our control
  • Requests denied by government agencies for legal reasons
  • Incorrect information provided by customer
  • Changes in government fee structures
  • Requests cancelled after submission to government agency

How to Request a Refund

  1. Email billing@municipalrecordsprocessing.com within 30 days of transaction
  2. Include your Request ID and transaction details
  3. Explain the reason for your refund request
  4. We will respond within 1 business day
  5. Approved refunds are processed within 5-10 business days

Dispute Resolution Process

Step 1: Direct Resolution (1-2 business days)

Contact our support team at support@municipalrecordsprocessing.com with your concern. Most issues are resolved at this stage.

Step 2: Formal Dispute (3-5 business days)

If unsatisfied with initial response, submit a formal dispute to disputes@municipalrecordsprocessing.com including:

  • Original request details
  • Previous correspondence
  • Desired resolution

Step 3: Management Review (5-7 business days)

Unresolved disputes are escalated to management for final review and resolution.

Chargeback Notice: We encourage customers to contact us before initiating a chargeback. We can often resolve issues faster through direct communication.

Cancellation Policy

Before Submission (Full Refund)

  • Requests can be cancelled within 1 hour of payment for full refund
  • Use the cancellation link in your confirmation email
  • Or email us immediately at support@municipalrecordsprocessing.com

After Submission (No Refund)

Once we submit your request to the government agency, cancellation is not possible as the agency has already begun processing.

Subscription Services

  • API subscriptions can be cancelled anytime
  • Access continues until end of billing period
  • No refunds for partial months
  • Unused request credits expire at end of billing period

Processing Times

Understanding our service helps set proper expectations:

  • Our Part: Request submission within 60 seconds (guaranteed)
  • Government Part: Document processing 48-72 hours typical (not guaranteed)
  • Total Time: Most requests completed within 3 business days

Quality Guarantee

We guarantee:

  • Accurate submission of your request information
  • Proper formatting according to agency requirements
  • Timely status updates as available
  • Secure handling of all documents
  • Professional support throughout the process
